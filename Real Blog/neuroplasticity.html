What is Neuroplasticity and a Growth Mindset? And how does it affect my learning.

Well neuroplasticity is the ability of the brain, where it can form and recognise synaptic connections, especially in response to learning or experiencing/following injury. And a growth mindest is when a person sees there own skills are upgradable. Meaning that if a problem arisis they see there current knowledge as a point of improvement. The way this influences my learning, is by teaching me its better to fail and learn from that failure. And if I apply that logic/mindset I will find that when I run into major problems I can adapt and overcome the problem.
